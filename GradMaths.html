Some study mathematics as a first degree, while others study mathematics in a "service course" as part of a numerate degree. So far there are just two articles that cover a couple of gaps in my undergraduate education. I hope to extend these to cover some revision notes on things I tend to find I have forgotten so much about every time I need them to solve a new problem. The articles are very informal, and I leave rigour to the standard texts.

  • GradMaths 2: Discrete Fourier Transforms and Sampling Theory
